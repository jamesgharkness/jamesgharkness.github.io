About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Electrical Engineering specializing in optics, photonics, and microfabrication. My research focuses on refractive index engineering, interferometry, and novel waveguide designs. I enjoy bridging experimental and computational approaches to solve engineering challenges.

Research

  • Design and simulation of integrated optical waveguides using FDFD methods.
  • Experimental characterization of refractive indices in SiO₂ and SiO thin films.
  • Development of interferometric systems for precision optical measurement.

Publications

  • Harkness, J. et al., “Measurement of Refractive Indices in Microfabricated Structures,” Optics Letters, 2025.
  • Harkness, J. et al., “Waveguide Mode Visualization via FDFD Simulation,” Journal of Photonics, 2024.
